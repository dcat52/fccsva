Valerie T. Mack, LCSW

Valerie Mack is a Licensed Clinical Social Worker with the State of Virginia. Valerie brings 20 years of experience working with children, adolescents, couples, and families from a variety of settings including private, public schools and community-based mental health facilities. Valerie provided therapeutic services to target issues including Anxiety, ADHD, Bipolar, Depression, Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder(PTSD) and Schizophrenia, and Substance Use Disorder.

Valerie earned her undergraduate and Graduate Degree in Social Work from Virginia Commonwealth University, and obtained certification in School Social Work to assist families in identifying special education placement and services for students. In addition, Valerie provides assessment and diagnostic evaluations for treatment purposes in children and adults.

Valerie has served as Clinical Coordinator for several Psychosocial Day Support program for adults and children with primary mental health diagnosis, dual diagnosis, and/or co-existing disorder.

Valerie has a passion for working with individuals and families in crisis. She believes each person has inherent resilience, and promotes a strength-based approach towards recovery. She focuses on the importance of building rapport with each member, and encourages empowerment to assist with reaching their potential.

Valerie is currently a member of the national Association of Social Workers(NASW) and the Association of Black Social Workers (ABSW).
