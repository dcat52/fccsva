Family Connections Counseling and Evaluation Services

What We Do

Family Connections Counseling and Evaluation Services has been meeting the mental health needs of the greater Richmond and surrounding areas since 1986. We are conveniently located in the heart of Chester, making us easily accessible to the counties of Chesterfield, Henrico, and Hanover, as well as Hopewell, Colonial Heights, and Richmond City. Our highly skilled clinical staff is committed to providing affordable, high quality mental health and wellness services to a diverse population across the lifespan. Our providers are passionate about what they do. We believe that with a little help our clients have the power within to make positive changes and live happier lives. Our customer satisfaction is evident through our number one referral source, previous clients, who refer their family members, friends, and colleagues.

Our clinical approach is what sets Family Connections apart from many other counseling practices. While we offer a wide variety of services including individual, marital, and family counseling, as well as comprehensive psychological evaluations, each client is given the opportunity to complete basic screening measures to help us better understand their problems. We believe it is important to understand the roots of our clients’ struggles in order to ensure a positive, counseling outcome.
